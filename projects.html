Projects

This RNA-seq project focuses on identifying differentially expressed mRNAs in diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL) using sequencing data from healthy and diseased dog models. The analysis aims to correlate miRNA expression with mRNA levels, validating findings through RT-qPCR, to advance diagnostics and personalized treatments for DLBCL.

This study analyzes SARS-CoV-2 variants in Nepal during the 2020-2021 pandemic waves. Using 127 sequences from the GISAID database, it identifies the alpha, delta, and kappa variants, with the delta variant emerging as dominant by July 2021. The findings emphasize the need for enhanced genomic surveillance and targeted public health policies.

Publication GitHub

This study investigates the antioxidant and anticancer properties of Psidium guajava L. essential oil. Through GC-MS analysis, 17 bioactive compounds were identified. In vitro tests revealed significant efficacy against breast cancer cells, while in silico studies suggest these compounds may act as selective estrogen receptor modulators, highlighting their therapeutic potential.
